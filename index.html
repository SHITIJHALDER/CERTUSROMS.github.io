About Us

We are a group of people who Have/Had Redmi 6 and 6A Devices. 6's and 6A's Firmware is almost the same. The difference occurs only in the Camera module. So Redmi 6's Rom for work in 6A and similarly 6A's Rom will work on Redmi 6. But here is catch, there can be different bugs on 1 of the device or in the both of them.

There are many ROMs out there, literally many. So the most popular Roms are Lineage OS, Pixel/Plus, CrDroid, Superior OS, Blaze OS, Project Lighthouse, Awaken OS, Dot OS, Nusantara OS, Spark OS and many more.
